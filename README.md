The Sales Management System is a web-based application designed to streamline and automate sales operations for businesses. It provides a structured platform for managing customers, salesmen, invoices, and items, ensuring efficient tracking of transactions and business performance.

Key features of the system include:

Customer Management – Store and manage customer details for better interaction and tracking.
Salesman Management – Keep records of sales representatives and their transactions.
Invoice Processing – Generate, store, and track invoices for each sale.
Item Management – Maintain an inventory of products or services available for sale.
This system enhances operational efficiency, reduces manual errors, and provides a clear overview of sales performance. It is ideal for businesses looking to improve their sales tracking, invoicing, and customer management processes. 

Also i am using Laravel for the backend with MariaDB for database and data storage . For the frontEnd I am using React (nextJs) / TypeScript and tailwind css.
